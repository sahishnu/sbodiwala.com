My Guru Hariprasad Swamiji
Tarre Laine Hu - Because of You

I am the person I am today because of my guru, Swamiji. Growing up I've had many teachers in life, as all students do. Each teacher is unique, providing their knowledge and skillset to educate students in a specific field of study. However, there aren't many teachers out there who can guide and inspire students in every subject of life. I've been fortunate enough to be blessed by Swamiji at just the age of three months. As I've grown up over the years, Swamiji has helped inspire and guide me every step of the way. From motivating me to achieve straight A's every school year, helping me decide on which field of study I should pursue, to even encouraging me to stay in the right company of friends, Swamiji has natured and supported me personally, academically, and socially. At every stage of life, Swamiji has been there to foster my potential. As I continue to grow and advance in my career, I will always know that I am who I am today, simply because of him.
