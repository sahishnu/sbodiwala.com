Leadership Youth Leader

There's nothing I love more than a challenge. I believe leadership begins with courage, but also passion. I love to inspire and motivate individuals to reach their potential.

Youth Leader at Yogi Divine Society

I'm blessed and fortunate to have been brought up in this organization. Having the company of many successfully people, I myself have learned the value and importance of great leadership. Through my development, I've been given the opportunity to lead and inspire youths (ages 15-35) from around the country to perform in the culture program during the annual Atmiya Youth Shibir. While the program usually runs during the end of July, preparation begins months in advance. I travel to many different cities including Columbus Ohio, Chicago Illinois, Houston Texas, and Parsippany New Jersey to meet with youths and prepare for this amazing event. Through the ups and downs, we remember that our purpose is to please our guru, Swamiji. It's an honor to have been selected to participate and lead in such an amazing event.
